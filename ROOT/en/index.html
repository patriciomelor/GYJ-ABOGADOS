ABOUT US

González Johnson & Cía has a solid track record of more than three decades in the national market, starting its operations as a consulting firm specialized in accounting and tax advisory services for a wide range of clients from various national industries. Over time, our company has focused its expertise in the tax field and has developed its legal area, achieving to serve foreign clients with operations in Chile and important national business groups.

We have a team of highly skilled and experienced professionals whose multidisciplinary training enables them to provide comprehensive and efficient service to our clients. In this sense, we understand that the current complexity of business and the constant evolution of regulations in accounting and taxation require the combination of both fields of knowledge to effectively identify our clients' needs and provide innovative and effective solutions.

In addition, in our efforts to be committed and responsible members of society, we have implemented a corporate social responsibility policy that includes various actions to support the community and environmental protection. Therefore, we support community projects and provide assistance to foundations that focus on social welfare and conservation of the natural environment.

PRACTICE AREAS

  • González Johnson & Co. is a company with experience in International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). It provides services and advice on the adoption and interpretation of these standards to its clients.
  • The preparation of accurate and complete accounting records is essential for proper control of a company or individual's finances. This includes the preparation of different types of records such as:
    • Income records, expense records, asset records, liability records
    • In addition to these records, the necessary balance sheets and subsidiary books will be prepared to have a complete and accurate view of the company's financial situation.
  • Accounting supervision to ensure accuracy and compliance with accounting and tax standards.
  • Advice and representation of foreign companies and establishment of a tax domicile in the country to fulfill tax obligations
  • Our financial audit service is a specialized solution that helps companies ensure proper management of their finances and compliance with accounting and tax standards. Our auditors, using specialized techniques, will review and evaluate the company's accounting records to determine if they are being handled appropriately. At the end of the process, a report with findings and recommendations will be issued to help the company correct financial issues and improve its management.
  • Our tax audit services are a specialized solution that helps companies ensure compliance with their tax obligations and minimize the risk of sanctions and fines. Through a thorough review of their tax records, our auditors will determine if the company is complying with all applicable tax regulations and laws. Additionally, we will identify opportunities to reduce costs and improve tax efficiency. At the end of the process, we will provide a detailed report with our findings and recommendations to help the company improve its tax situation. With our tax audit services, companies can be sure to comply with their tax obligations and reduce the risk of legal problems.
  • Our internal audit services are a valuable tool for companies seeking to ensure the efficiency, effectiveness, and compliance of their internal operations. Through a thorough review of their processes, policies, and internal controls, our auditors will identify areas for improvement and potential risks.
  • Our urban and agricultural title studies services allow for a thorough analysis of ownership titles, including verification of their legality and regularity. In addition, we provide services for drafting and processing all types of public and private relevant instruments, to provide comprehensive advice in the real estate area.
  • In the corporate field, we prepare all types of documents, from incorporations to the dissolution of a company, including transformations, mergers, divisions, etc.
  • In the civil area, support is provided with the preparation of all types of contracts.
  • Legal advice in labor matters, especially in the negotiation stage, calculations, and severance proposals that lead to consensual evictions.
  • Our tax defense services offer comprehensive assistance in the management of tax controversies. We offer pre-defense in inspection or citation processes before the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), to help clients reduce the risk of fines and sanctions. Additionally, we provide a court processing service in Tax and Customs Courts and appellate courts, to ensure clients receive a fair resolution in their cases. Our specialists are highly trained in tax law and have a broad knowledge of tax regulation. With our tax defense services, clients can be confident in receiving professional assistance in the management of their tax controversies.
  • Processing of various civil lawsuits, especially those for billing or other executive titles and those for the prescription of municipal patents.
  • Defense in labor lawsuits for unjustified dismissal, labor protection, collection of benefits, etc.
  • Our services include a comprehensive approach to ensure tax compliance and improve our clients' fiscal efficiency. We offer:
    • Tax compliance and diagnosis: to identify areas for improvement in the company's tax situation and ensure compliance with tax regulations and laws.
    • Permanent consulting: to provide constant advice and keep up to date with changing tax regulations.
    • Due diligence: to evaluate a company's tax and financial situation before a transaction.
    • Corporate reorganizations and business structures: to help companies optimize their tax structure and maximize fiscal efficiency.
    • International taxation: to provide advice on international tax regulations and minimize the risk of legal problems.
    • Wealth Management and Family Office Advice for Individuals: to help individuals manage their wealth and minimize the tax impact.
  • Our deferred tax and transfer pricing service is designed to help companies. We offer expert advice on implementing deferred tax strategies, allowing companies to defer tax payment until a future date. Additionally, we provide transfer pricing consultation to ensure that transactions between related entities are conducted at fair prices and in accordance with international tax regulations.
  • Our services are customized to meet the specific needs of our clients, with the goal of maximizing their tax efficiency and minimizing the risk of legal issues.
  • We offer contract drafting and settlement services to help companies protect their legal interests and ensure compliance with their obligations. Our highly trained lawyers and professionals prepare precise and comprehensive contracts that address all possible contingencies.
  • Our payroll settlement process refers to a set of procedures that include social security contributions, overtime registration, application of the Unique Tax, preparation of the annual payroll statement, Honorary Declaration, application of approved voluntary discounts, among other relevant aspects.
  • We are committed to maintaining confidentiality in the handling of private payroll, a personalized and confidential service intended for those clients who need a specialized focus on Payroll Settlemen
  • We offer payroll outsourcing services to help companies optimize their payroll operations and comply with Labor and tax regulations. Our highly trained professionals provide payroll administration services to companies of all sizes, regardless of their industry or location. With our help, companies can reduce compliance risk and meet both current and future payroll needs. This way, companies can focus on their main business and leave payroll management in our hands
